Welcome to Contracts!

This is the website for the gg2 contracts plugin! You can find the source code for the plugin on Github.

What's this?

Like most Gang Garrison things, GG2 Contracts are largely inspired from their original TF2 counterpart.
You complete missions, some easy some hard, and earn points based on how difficult they are.

The plugin keeps track of your results, and sends the data to this website,
which grants you points (so-called "canadian dollars") depending on how many contracts you complete!
Collect a bunch of points, and spend them on exclusive cosmetics, such as medals or a special hat!

There are more than 20 types of contracts! Kill enemies, set them on fire, capture objectives, heal your allies without dying...

How do I join?

Find a game server that runs the contracts plugin (or host one yourself!). You will be greeted with a floating
portrait of Ms. Paula in the corner of your screen. Click it!

You will be greeted with a (basic) sign up form:



After clicking "Yes", an account will automatically be created for you. From there, you can just play the game!

You can choose to click "Not Now" to make the icon disappear until you restart the game.
Clicking "Never" will make the icon disappear forever, but you can still sign up later from the plugin options menu.

When your account is created, you are given a login key (or user key), which is saved in your gg2.ini .
If you want to log into the same account from a different computer, just copy over the entire file;
or if you want to be specific, copy the user_key under [Contracts] .


When joining a server, you will be given a list of contracts to fulfill.
You can find them in the Contracts menu, from the in-game menu.

How do I see my progress?

In game, a small tracker in the corner of the screen will show your current contracts.
The tracker will only show the contracts that you can complete as your current class.



In the game menu, you can see all your current contracts, how many points they're worth, and how close you are to completing them.

You can find your total points, as well as everyone else's, in the general table on the users page.

How do I get new contracts?

Contracts are completed when a map ends.
When you complete a contract, it is immediately replaced with a new random contract.

You cannot choose what contracts you get, but you can choose a main class for your account.
This will make it more likely that your new generated contracts will have to do with the class you've chosen!
Your default main class will be the Rocketman, but you can change it anytime by going to your profile page, which you can also access with a link in the Contracts menu in game.
